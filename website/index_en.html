Features

Transitioning to Professional & Organized Management

We built the software to help you centralize all information in one smart, professional, and easy-to-use system.

Moving to Smart Digital Management

A clear, colorful calendar that shows you everything happening in the boarding visually, pleasantly, and effortlessly.

Full Team Synchronization

No more noisy WhatsApp groups with hundreds of messages. Ongoing documentation of everything happening in the boarding, including food, medications, and behavior, all organized in the log.

Team Management
